HOW TO PLAY

  • Restore the damaged circuit by reconnecting the data stream source to it’s destination.
  • Click on two circuit blocks to swap their locations and make a path.
  • Dead circuits are too badly damaged to be moved. You’ll have to work around them.
  • If the timer runs out and the charge cannot advance, the circuit board will be fried!
